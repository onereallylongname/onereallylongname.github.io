Naturshell

A product development project.

A short introduction

Naturshell was a project for the discipline of E.D.P. (Engineering and product development). The objective of this project was the creation of an innovative product related to bicycles. This work lasted for one semester, for a four person group. SolidWorks was used for CAD development.
