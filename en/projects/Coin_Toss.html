Coin Toss

A simulator made with unity.

A coin tossing simulator

In response to "How thick is a three-sided coin?", a video posted by standupmaths on youtube, I decided to dedicate an afternoon to help in the demand of rolling coins. The challenge is to find out the ratio between the diameter and thickness of the coin that makes it equally likely to land "face", "crown" or "sideways."

The Simulator was written in C #, using the Unity engine. Instead of face or crown the sides of the coins are blue or red, and are counted automatically by the program.

There now two versions of the project. For more info check the Docs link below.
